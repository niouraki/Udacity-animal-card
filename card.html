The Great A'Tuin

Great A'Tuin, the World Turtle, was the gigantic turtle upon whose back the Discworld was carried through space

  • Scientific Name: Great A'Tuin
  • Average Length: Unknown
  • Average Lifespan: Immortal
  • Habitat: Space

Great A'Tuin is a fantasy animal, which appears in Terry Pratchet's Discworld book series. It carries the world of Discworld on its back. Many things remain unknown about Great A'Tuin, including its gender and desires. These are matters of constant speculation by philosophers, mystics and theologians. It is known that A'Tuin was not the only World Turtle, however.
